# Shoulder Surfing Reloaded: Camera Fixes & Additions

A few fixes and additions for Shoulder Surfing Reloaded when used with Epic Fight, Better Lockon, and Iron's Spells. Forge 1.20.1.

## What it does

- Press O to cycle right shoulder, left shoulder, overhead. SSR can't do that combo on its own.
- Hides the vanilla crosshair while you're locked on, so you don't get two crosshairs at once.
- Keeps your shoulder offset during lock-on instead of recentering the camera on you.
- Smoothly transitions between presets while locked on.
- No camera-snapback after you release lock-on.
- Sprinting backwards no longer makes the camera chase your movement direction. Stays on the enemy.
- Body actually follows the crosshair while casting Iron's Spells, so projectiles spawn where you're aiming.

## Requires

Minecraft 1.20.1, Forge 47+, Shoulder Surfing Reloaded 4.22.0+, Epic Fight 20.14.1+. Better Lockon and Iron's Spells are optional.

## Config

One option, `cameraOverheadOffsetY`, for how high the overhead preset sits. Everything else uses SSR's own config.
